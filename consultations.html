Interested in a herbalist consultation?

I offer a variety of ways to connect with plants. Through a consult we will figure out what works best for your situation. We will start with a 1,5 hour interview where we talk about different aspects of you and your situation. Afterwards I create a treatment plan and give recommendations. Working with plant medicine is different than allopathic medicine. Most often it works in different time spectrums depending on the person, and therefore I recommend you to commit for at least 3- 4 months if possible. I work from a standpoint that everyone has the right to heal, and offer consults on a sliding scale. Let me know what works for you and we take it from there.
